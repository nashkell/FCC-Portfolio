About me

The process for design starts with concept, drawing, development, and execution through presentation. Embracing technology both old and new, I see myself as a passionate individual that can inform, inspire, connect, and create.

My body of work currently includes pieces ranging from website design to motion graphics and print. Located in TriBeCa, New York; I am a graphic designer seeking work with an emphasis on typography, information, wayfinding, interface design, motion graphics, and other forms of visual and audial communication and problem solving.

I am confident with HTML, CSS, jQuery. I am proficient in Adobe Indesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects with some audio editing and mixing knowledge. I also have some experience in print and digital design.
